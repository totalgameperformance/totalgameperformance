The Science

This isn't motivational content.
It's applied sports psychology.

Cortisol Recovery

Unmanaged stress hormones take 6–8 minutes to clear after a mistake. Players trained in focus control recover in under 60 seconds. The Playbook trains this exact mechanism.

Visualization Research

A landmark study found athletes who only visualized free throws improved 23% — almost identical to those who physically practiced. Your brain can't distinguish vivid imagination from reality.

Emotion Labelling

UCLA research shows that simply naming an emotion ("I'm anxious") reduces its intensity by up to 50%. This is one of the specific tools built on Day 2.
